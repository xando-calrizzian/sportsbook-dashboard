Glossary & methodology
Official paper ledger
The canonical cohort paper record for this strategy family. It is backfilled from May 12, 2026 and carried forward daily.
1 unit
One dollar of paper bankroll. No real money was placed. All figures on this page are hypothetical paper units.
Total value
Bankroll in play plus accumulated savings. Equivalent to starting bankroll plus lifetime net P&L.
Paper active
The portion of a strategy value assigned to currently open paper rows. Savings cannot be lost by pending rows.
Savings
End-of-day rake. On profitable days, 20% of the closing bankroll moves to savings, capped so next-day bankroll never falls below today’s start.
EV/$
Expected value per dollar at stake. Computed as decimal odds times estimated win probability minus 1. Filtered at each strategy’s minimum EV/$ floor.
Win %
Wins divided by settled paper rows. PUSH and VOID rows do not count.
Eff
Efficiency: lifetime paper P&L divided by lifetime assigned stake. ROI per paper unit risked.
Odds source
API: median across DK, FD, MGM, Caesars from The Odds API, vig-stripped. HC: fallback to the capper’s quoted odds when API does not match a pick.
Top-K
Each strategy is allowed at most max_bets_per_day paper rows. Surplus picks ranked by Kelly weight are cut.
Chalk filter
Picks priced below the strategy’s american_odds_floor are dropped before sizing.
Per-row cap
No single paper row exceeds 15% of bankroll. Excess weight is redistributed.
Streak
Consecutive trading days the portfolio total moved in the same direction.
